MoFR

MoFR is our service for mobile offers and ancillary services. Customer club, membership cards, targeted offers and webshop? Everything fits in one app on your mobile.

In addition to this, MoFR can include a web page, with or without login. Contact us to know more.

CheckEasy

CheckEasy is a mobile checklist system that is used for administering projects in the field. The flow of the project is seamless and without unnecessary interruptions.

The project manager has a real-time overview whether they are in the office or out in the field. CheckEasy allows projects to be kept paperless and easily accessible to everyone involved through documentation directly in the app.

Possum

Possum is the POS system for you who develop with your business. Our versions for Retail and Restaurant cover the requirements of today and tomorrow. Our development team works hard to constantly be at the forefront of new solutions.

Wherever you are, you can keep track of how things are going. Update and change the content of your business.

SMS

In today's connected society, SMS has advantages that other media lack. Most SMS messages are read within a few minutes, which means that whether it is information or a concrete offer to be delivered, it reaches the chosen target group quickly and easily.

SMS is currently used for subscriptions, offers and reminders, for example. We make sure that the right person receives the right information at the right time.
